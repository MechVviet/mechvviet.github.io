ABOUT THE INSTITUTE



Vidya Vikas Institute of Engineering and Technology (VVIET) was started in the Year 1997, in pursuit of developing Science and Technology through Teaching, Research and Development. The Institute delivers best-quality Education and Training to mould the technophiles into highly professional world-class Engineers.

VVIET is recognized by the Government of Karnataka as well as the All India Council of Technical Education (AICTE), New Delhi, affiliated to the Visvesvaraya Technological University (VTU), Belagavi. The Institute is governed by the Vidya Vikas Educational Trust (VVET).

The Institute provides a top class and comprehensive learning atmosphere for studies. The six major streams of study of Under Graduate Programme in Engineering include,

  1. Computer Science and Engineering (CS&E)
  2. Information Science and Engineering (IS&E)
  3. Electronics and Communication Engineering (E&C)
  4. Electrical and Electronics Engineering (E&E)
  5. Mechanical Engineering (ME), and
  6. Civil Engineering (CE)

The Institute also offers Post Graduate (PG) Programmes, namely,

  1. Master of Computer Applications (MCA)
  2. Master of Business Administration (MBA)
  3. M. Tech. in Digital Electronics and Communication Systems, and
  4. M.Tech. in Production Technology.
